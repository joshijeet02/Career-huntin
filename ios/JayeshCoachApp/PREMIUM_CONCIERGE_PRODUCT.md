# Premium Concierge Product Plan

## What a $1,000/month client expects

- Daily personalized guidance that is practical in less than 10 minutes.
- Proactive nudges when they are busy and likely to disengage.
- Emotionally intelligent support for both strategic and relationship pressure.
- Measurable progress over 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, and 1 year.
- Privacy and trust (confidentiality, retention controls, encrypted storage).

## What a $10,000/month client expects

- Decision intelligence at chairman-level quality for high-stakes choices.
- Evidence-backed recommendations, not generic advice.
- Predictive risk alerts (burnout, relationship strain, execution drift).
- Concierge-level consistency in tone, structure, and accountability.
- Institutional impact: not only personal coaching, but leadership-system leverage.

5. Calendar-aware context in every turn.
6. Last 5 memory themes in every turn for personalization continuity.
7. Secure backend auth + encrypted conversation storage + retention controls.
8. Research intelligence endpoints and premium tier endpoints.
9. Strict persona policy pack for consistent structured response style.
